My opponent claims this will foster innovation. Open-sourcing would lead to chaotic development, rife with errors, biases, and security flaws that could have devastating real-world consequences. Allowing the development of advanced AI to proliferate without control shifts an immense amount of influence – and potentially destructive capability – away from those who understand its implications and towards those most likely to exploit it.
