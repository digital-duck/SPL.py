**pint status:** `OK`

## Interpretation

A car starts from rest and accelerates steadily at 2.5 m/s² for 12 seconds — how fast is it going at the end?

Using the kinematic formula **v = a × t**, the final speed is **108.0 km/h** (equivalent to 30.0 m/s). This is roughly highway cruising speed, reached in just 12 seconds — a fairly brisk acceleration.

Final answer: 108.0
