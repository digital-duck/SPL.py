As Winter's chill begins to fade,
The earth awakens from its shade,
And Spring, with all her beauty, stays,
Bringing new life to winter's gray days.

The sun shines bright, with warmth and light,
Awakening buds, and flowers in sight,
The air is filled with sweet perfume,
As nature's palette starts to bloom.

The trees regain their vibrant hue,
Their leaves rustling softly, anew,
The birds return, with songs so sweet,
Their melodies, a symphony to greet.

The world awakens, fresh and green,
A time of renewal, pure and serene,
Hope eternal, in every breeze,
As Spring's magic brings its gentle ease.

In this season of rebirth and growth,
We find our spirits take new flight,
Our hearts filled with the promise of new life,
As Spring's beauty shines, a radiant light.

So let us cherish these fleeting days,
And bask in Spring's warm, golden rays,
For in her splendor, we find our peace,
A time to reflect, and let our souls release.
