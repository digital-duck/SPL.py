## Interpretation

This problem asks for the fixed monthly payment on a $10,000 loan at 6% annual interest (compounded monthly) over 5 years.

The monthly payment is **$193.33**. Over 60 payments, you'll pay a total of $11,599.80 — meaning $1,599.80 goes toward interest.

This was calculated using the standard **loan amortization formula**, which applies compound interest monthly and spreads payments evenly so each installment covers both accruing interest and a portion of the principal.

Final answer: 193.33

M_rounded = M.quantize(Decimal('0.01'), rounding=ROUND_HALF_UP)
