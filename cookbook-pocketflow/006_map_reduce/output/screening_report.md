## 5. Methodology

Resumes were evaluated by an LLM-based scoring pipeline against four weighted criteria: **relevant work experience**, **technical skills**, **educational background**, and **communication clarity**. Each criterion was assessed as Met, Partially Met, or Not Met; the composite score was normalized to a 10-point scale. The qualification threshold was set at **7.0/10**. This approach enables consistent, criterion-anchored evaluation at scale while eliminating inter-reviewer variance. Scores should be treated as a structured triage signal — not a hiring decision — and all advancing candidates require human validation in subsequent interview stages.
